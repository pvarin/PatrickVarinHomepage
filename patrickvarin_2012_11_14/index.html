MY STORY

Hi! My name is Patrick. I'm 20 years old, studying Engineering at Olin College, and having the time of my life. I built this website to share what I've been up to over the last couple years. Take a look around and let me know what you think!

If you haven't heard of Olin College, here's my shameless plug: Olin is a small engineering school that was built in order to redefine engineering education, and now it's getting international recognition as a leader in technical education. My major is formally "Engineering with a Concentration in Controls". Basically, this means that I study techniques that are used to control complex systems. I answer questions like "How do you move the tail of a robotic fish to make it swim efficiently?" or "How do you control a fleet of autonomous boats to work together to perform a task?"

When I'm not wrapped up in one of my projects, I spend my time running around outside, climbing trees, playing soccer, or painting.
