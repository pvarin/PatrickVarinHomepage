E:Controls

My major is formally "Engineering with a Concentration in Controls". In short, I study techniques that are used to control complicated systems. I answer questions like "How do you move the tail of a robotic fish to make it swim?" or "How do you control a fleet of autonomous boats to perform a specific task?"
